About Heart & Petal

Delivering love, one bouquet at a time

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Heart & Petal was born from a simple belief: flowers have the power to transform moments into memories, and every delivery should be an expression of love and care.

What started as a small local flower shop has blossomed into a trusted online destination for premium floral arrangements, romantic gifts, and heartfelt surprises. Our journey has been guided by our passion for beauty, quality, and the joy of helping people celebrate life's most precious moments.

Today, we serve thousands of customers across the country, but we've never forgotten our roots. Every arrangement is still hand-crafted with the same attention to detail and personal touch that made our first bouquet special.
